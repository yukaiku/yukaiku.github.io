About me

Salesforce Technical Consultant with a background in web and mobile development. Since starting my career in Salesforce I have been involved in small scale experience cloud sites to large scale financial services projects that has developed both my functional and technical skills.

In the areas of technical, I have worked on developing using LWC to create visually aesthetic components that integrates well in both experience site and screen flows. Other technical skills I picked up along my journey are writing apex handlers for triggers and LWC components to allow customer to be able to identify key information from an email based on the subject and querying for records to render on LWC components.

Despite being a technical consultant I also had my fair share of functional development, ranging from setting up permission sets to the right user to complex tasks such as routing email to case to the appropriate case management teams to handle and resolve.
